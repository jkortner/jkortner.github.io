About

I am an assistant diplômé at the IDHEAP under the supervision of Giuliano Bonoli and Rafael Lalive (HEC). In my PhD project I study the use of predictive algorithms in the delivery of public employment services. Before starting my PhD I worked as a data scientist and graduated with a MSc from the University of Konstanz.
