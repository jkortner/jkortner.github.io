About

I am a PhD student at the IDHEAP of the University of Lausanne. In my thesis projects, I study the interplay of algorithmic predictions and bureaucratic discretion, using administrative data, field experiments and causal machine learning. Before starting my PhD, I worked as a data scientist in the Lufthansa Group and graduated with a MSc in Social and Economic Data Analysis from the University of Konstanz.
