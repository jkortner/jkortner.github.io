About

I am a PhD student at the IDHEAP in Lausanne under the
supervision of Giuliano Bonoli and Rafael Lalive. I am interested in how street-level bureaucrats use information from predictive algorithms. Before starting my PhD, I worked as a data scientist and graduated with a MSc from the University of Konstanz.

Research
