About

I am a fourth-year PhD student at the IDHEAP of the University of Lausanne. In my thesis projects, I study the interplay of algorithms and bureaucratic discretion, using administrative data, data from a field experiment, and causal machine learning. During Fall 2024, I will be based at the Wilf Family Department of Politics at New York University, hosted by Tara Slough.
