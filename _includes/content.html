About

I am a PhD student at the IDHEAP of the University of Lausanne under the supervision of Giuliano Bonoli and Rafael Lalive. I am interested in the use of predictions in the social sciences and for social policy. Before starting my PhD, I worked as a data scientist in the Lufthansa Group and graduated with a MSc from the University of Konstanz.
