About

I am a PhD student at the University of Lausanne. In October, I will join the Collegio Carlo Alberto as a postdoc. I study welfare institutions, with a focus on how discretion, information, and incentives interact in the distribution of benefits and services.

My research has been supported by a Postdoc.Mobility fellowship, a Mobi.Doc grant, and by the Horowitz Foundation.
